Three Milk Cake

Description

Sponge cake soaked in a mixture of three kinds of milk, topped with whipped cream and strawberries

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of heavy whipping cream
  • 1 can (14 ounce) of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can (12 ounce) of evaporated milk
  • 1 cup of whipped cream
  • 1 cup of sliced fresh strawberries

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 C)
  2. Grease a 10x15-inch baking dish
  3. Beat egg whites in a large mixing bowl with an electric mixer on high until stiff peaks form
  4. Gradually beat in sugar until mixture is glossy
  5. Beat in egg yolks, one at a time, combining each yolk before adding the next
  6. Reduce mixer speed to medium and add flour, about 1 tablespoon at a time, to the mixture, beating continuously
  7. Beat in baking powder, milk, and vanilla extract
  8. Pour batter into prepared baking dish
  9. Bake in the preheated oven until cake is lightly browned (about 25 minutes)
  10. Let cake cool for 15 minutes.
  11. Pour 1 cup cream, sweetened condensed milk, and evaporated milk in a blender and pulse several times until well blended
  12. Pour three-milk mixture evenly over the cake
  13. Refrigerate cake until cold and the milk mixture has soaked in, at least 1 hour
  14. Cut in squares and top each serving with a dollop of whipped cream and a few sliced strawberries.
