Baked Chicken Wings

Description

Easy and crispy portion of 10 oven baked chicken wings recipe that takes 1 hour to cook

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 3 cloves of pressed garlic
  • 2 teaspoons of chilli powder
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 10 chicken wings
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 C)
  2. Combine seasoning in a large, resealable bag; seal and shake to combine
  3. Add the chicken wings, reseal and shake to coat
  4. Arrange the chicken wings on a baking sheet
  5. Cook in the preheated oven for 1 hour
